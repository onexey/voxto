# Hotkeys

Voxto starts with **F9** as the default recording shortcut.

To change it, open **Preferences → General**. The current shortcut is shown in the hotkey field, and **Record shortcut** listens for the next shortcut you press.

- Press a single key such as `F9` to use it on its own.
- Press a combination such as `Ctrl+F9` or `Ctrl+Shift+R` to save that combination.
- Click **Cancel recording** to stop listening and keep the current shortcut.

The selected shortcut is used for both hotkey modes:

- **Toggle** — press once to start, press again to stop.
- **Push-to-talk** — hold to record, release to stop.
